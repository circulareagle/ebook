1. Introduction

Circular Eagle ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle information collected through our website and newsletter services.

Circular Eagle does not process, store, or see your financial information. All eBook purchases are handled securely through our partner, Selar.

When you click "Purchase," you are redirected to Selar's secure environment. Your transaction is subject to Selar's Privacy Policy. We only receive confirmation of your purchase to ensure content delivery or affiliate tracking.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your email address exclusively to:

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access to your email data. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send.
